Welcome to Läleony Hotels

Selamat datang di Läleony Hotel

Läleony Hotel and Resort is located on the white sandy beach of Alissa, with beautiful sunsets. Laleony Hotel has direct access to the famous Alissa Beach, and only a 5-10 minute walk to the shopping mall and entertainment area. It is only 3 miles from the Asal Terbang International Airport and 6 miles from the city of Pochinki.

Läleony Hotel and Resort offers two distinct design styles in its accommodations with its refreshing hospitality concept-Greenwood Wing and Seaside Wing, with a total of 400 guest rooms, family rooms and suites. Greenwood Wing has a traditional guest room style. Each room has a private balcony. It is specially designed for guests looking for the natural atmosphere of the green forest. In addition, the modern style of Seaside Wing creates a modern atmosphere, with various views of the swimming pool, sea or garden from the room.
